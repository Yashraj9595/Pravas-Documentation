Introduction to Pravas

What is Pravas?

Pravas is an innovative mobile application and web platform designed to transform the tourism experience in Jharkhand. It combines cutting-edge technologies like AI, Blockchain, and AR/VR to create a seamless, personalized, and secure tourism ecosystem.

The app aims to promote eco-tourism and cultural exploration while supporting local communities and preserving the rich heritage of the region.

The Problem

Tourism in Jharkhand faces several challenges:

  • Lack of proper digital infrastructure for tourists
  • Fragmented tourism services with no central platform
  • Limited awareness about local attractions and cultural experiences
  • Difficulty in discovering authentic local products and services
  • Language barriers for domestic and international tourists
  • Concerns about security and authenticity of services

Our Solution

Pravas addresses these challenges by providing:

  • A comprehensive digital platform for all tourism needs
  • AI-powered personalized itineraries based on user preferences
  • Real-time navigation and transport information
  • Immersive AR/VR experiences of key attractions
  • Blockchain-secured payments and verified listings
  • A marketplace connecting tourists with local artisans and service providers
  • Multilingual support through an AI-driven chatbot

Through these features, Pravas aims to create a sustainable tourism ecosystem that benefits both tourists and local communities.
